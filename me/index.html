Motivation to choose NTU DP

I compare NTU DP only with Dnipro universities, because I have some info around Dnipro and I don`t know situation in others Ukrainian or abroad cities. Of course, if you come to any university you have to make an effort, because no one will pull you over and your life is in your hands. Just keep this in your head.
I think that you hear about problem when lecturer is prejudiced to students who receive a scholarship and try to take away this title. Me friend is a student of another Dnipro university and he told me that they have similar situation when students with budget study meet with pressure. During 2 years of study I`ve never faced with proper situation. Lecturers and teachers are equally loyal to all students.
I noticed in previous paragraph that teachers are loyal. I want to expand this topic. The loads in university are not so big that they could be. So you can calmly develop yourself in another spheres in your free time.
